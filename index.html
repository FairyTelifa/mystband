NEW ALBUM DROP

This will be their first ever album. The theme of this album will be about their title song "Without." The song gives the story of a woman who is dealing with despair and hopelessness coming from a loss. We have not been informed what the loss is as they want it to be a suprise until you listen to the song. But by the end of the song, she will embrace the despair that she is feeling and will hopefully regain her will to live or see the light that she saw as a child.

ABOUT THE BAND

The alternative rock band MYST wants to bring the old sound of rock back to life but to a younger crowd. They influences include the classic alt-rock bands, L7, Garbage, Radiohead, and The Cranberries. The band and label feel they share a similar alternative rock crowd.
